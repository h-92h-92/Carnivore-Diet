Carnivore Breakfast Pizza Recipe

This keto pizza features a base made from the Carnivore Flatbread, which is incredibly easy to make with only 3 ingredients! After using it for a delicious Keto Cream Spinach Stromboli, I've now repurposed it as the crust for a hearty Carnivore Breakfast Pizza. It’s perfect for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Crust

To make the crust for this carnivore pizzacarnivore pizza, prepare one batch of the Carnivore Flatbread. Here’s what you need:

  • Eggs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Mozzarella
  • Spices

Spread the batter into an oval or rectangle shape of about 13×11 inches and bake.

Tip: You can make this crust ahead of time. If you want to bake the pizza for breakfast, prepare the crust the night before. In the morning, just add the toppings and bake!

Sauce

Top off your breakfast pizza with a creamy sour cream sauce. To make the sauce, whisk together the following ingredients:

  • Sour cream
  • Heavy cream
  • Pressed or minced garlic
  • Salt
  • Shredded cheeses (mozzarella, cheddar, or gouda)

Spread this sauce over the baked crust, then add your choice of meat.

Meat

This breakfast pizza tastes amazing with crispy bacon. However, you can also use other meats such as:

  • Chopped deli ham
  • Cooked breakfast sausage
  • A combination of different meats

Feel free to experiment and find your favorite combination!
